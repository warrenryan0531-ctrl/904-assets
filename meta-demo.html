pages_show_list Permission Active

Lists all Facebook Pages managed by the authorized user. 904 Digital Media uses this to discover which Pages a client has admin access to when they connect their account.
pages_show_list
GET graph.facebook.com/v25.0/me/accounts

pages_read_user_content Permission Active

Reads posts, comments, and ratings on a client's Facebook Page. Used to surface user-generated content for moderation and reporting dashboards.
pages_read_user_content
GET graph.facebook.com/v25.0/1175505708976931/feed?fields=id,message,created_time

pages_manage_posts Permission Active

Publishes posts to a client's Facebook Page on their behalf. This is the core posting capability of the 904 Digital Media content management workflow.
pages_manage_posts
GET graph.facebook.com/v25.0/1175505708976931/feed?fields=id,message,created_time,story

pages_read_engagement Permission Active

Retrieves engagement metrics and analytics insights for client Pages. Powers the reporting dashboard showing reach, impressions, and engagement over time.
pages_read_engagement
GET graph.facebook.com/v25.0/1175505708976931/insights?metric=page_impressions,page_engaged_users&period=day

pages_manage_engagement Permission Active

Allows replying to comments and moderating user engagement on client Pages. Used to respond to customer messages and comments on behalf of clients.
pages_manage_engagement
GET graph.facebook.com/v25.0/1175505708976931/feed?fields=id,message,comments{id,message,created_time}

instagram_basic Permission Active

Reads basic profile information for a client's Instagram Business Account. Used to verify account connection and populate the client dashboard.
instagram_basic
GET graph.facebook.com/v25.0/17841416495061870?fields=id,username,name,followers_count,media_count,biography

instagram_content_publish Permission Active

Publishes photos and videos to a client's Instagram Business Account. The primary posting permission for the Instagram content workflow.
instagram_content_publish
GET graph.facebook.com/v25.0/17841416495061870/media?fields=id,media_type,timestamp,caption

business_management
